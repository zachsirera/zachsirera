Zach Sirera

Hi, I'm Zach. I'm a chemical engineer living in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.


I have always had an interest in computer programming and data. I began teaching myself programming and computer science skills in 2018, starting with various small Python projects. In May of 2018 I enrolled in Harvard's CS50, where I worked with C and eventually back to the Python and the Web Development framework Flask.


At this time I also started picking up more sophisticated side projects, the main one being the full-stack Android and iOS app Sembli. Sembli is a group-management app that allows users to check into events based on geolocation of their smartphone. This app was built with Ionic v4, Angular 6, and AWS.


In August of 2019 I enrolled in a M.S. in Computer Science at Georgia Tech.
